About

With 35 years in the field of Social Services, Cynthia has held various positions, ranging from Case Manager to Director of programs. One common thread among these roles was that they all allowed her to fulfill her life's mission: helping improve the lives of individuals, from children to adults. This has been Cynthia's way of staying connected to what truly matters—and for her, that's family.

Born in New Haven, Connecticut, Cynthia later moved to New York City, where she spent her childhood and young adulthood growing up in public housing. She vividly remembers looking up at the tall buildings and thinking, "I wonder what life has to offer on the other side of that rainbow."

Now semi-retired, Cynthia resides in Jacksonville, Florida, with her family and runs her Certified Life Coaching practice. She can be reached for advice at (904) 955-5990.

Work

Cynthia credits her success to the unwavering support of her kind and loving family, which enabled her to earn two college degrees. She has an extensive career spanning over 30 years in Social Services, a field that has taken her across the globe. As an Assistant Manager with the International Division of the American Red Cross, she had the vital responsibility of establishing a fully operational emergency communication program for the armed forces and their families while they were stationed in West Germany.

Upon returning to the U.S., Cynthia continued this work as a Director for the Red Cross, supporting military installations in New Jersey. She later expanded her career by working with the State of Florida and various nonprofit agencies in social services. These roles allowed her to develop strong advocacy, communication, coordination, and assessment skills, making her highly resourceful in linking clients to essential services.

Following this phase of her career, Cynthia, her husband, and their newborn daughter relocated to Jacksonville, Florida. Since then, she has contributed to numerous state and private projects aimed at developing North Florida.

Reflecting on her journey, Cynthia states, "After so many years of providing platforms to assist individuals in their journey and receiving numerous awards and accolades, it's no wonder I became a Certified Life Coach."
